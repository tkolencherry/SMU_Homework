Latitude vs Temperature



There is a strong positive correlation (r = 0.77) between latitude and temperature in the southern hemisphere and a strong negative correlation (r= -0.89) between latitude and temperature in the northern hemisphere. This relationship makes sense since the closer to the equator a country is the more direct sunlight it receives from the sun due to the Earth's tilt on its axis.
